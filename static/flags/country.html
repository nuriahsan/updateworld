Description

In FOTW, in general, ISO 3166 codes are used to file pages and images. Below is a list of names of entities from ISO 3166, linked to their flag pages. Some locations without ISO 3166-1 codes are included in the list as well. These are provided purely as a convenience to our visitors for locating flags, by clicking such names. Their presence does not imply anything about their diplomatic status.
